Core Features

Invoices - Create, send, track, mark paid, download PDF
Payments - Record payments (cash, card, check, transfer), track history
Partial Payments - Pay invoices in multiple installments, track remaining balance
Quotations - Create treatment estimates, convert to invoices
Payment Plans - Set up installment agreements with schedules
Procedure-Based Billing - Auto-generate invoices from completed treatments/appointments
Reports - Revenue trends, outstanding balance, overdue invoices, tax summary
Insurance Claims - Track insurance reimbursements
Credit Notes - Handle refunds and overpayments

Database Tables

procedures - Dental procedures (name, code, cost, category, tooth_number)
insurance_claims - Claims (invoice_id, provider, claim_number, status, reimbursement)
credit_notes - Refunds (invoice_id, reason, amount, status)
financial_settings - Cabinet info, invoice templates, email settings

Calculation Logic
Invoice Status Flow:
- Draft → Issued → (Partial Payment) → (Partial Payment) → Paid
- Overdue status: If due_date < today and status != Paid

Remaining Balance Calculation:
- Remaining = Total Amount - Sum of All Payments

Payment Recording:
- If payment_amount >= remaining_balance → Status = Paid
- If payment_amount > 0 and < remaining_balance → Status = Partial_Paid
- If payment_amount > remaining_balance → Create credit note for overpayment

Procedure-Based Invoice:
- Auto-link treatments to invoice items
- Cost = procedure cost from catalog or custom override
- Auto-calculate total from procedures
- Mark procedure as billed after invoice issued

Main Components Interaction
Workflow 1: Procedure-Based Billing with Partial Payments
1. Complete appointment/treatment
2. Auto-generate invoice from treatment
3. Send invoice to patient
4. Patient makes partial payment
5. Record payment (remaining balance auto-calculated)
6. Send reminder for balance
7. Patient pays remaining
8. Mark invoice as paid

Workflow 2: Quotation → Invoice → Partial Payments
1. Create quotation for procedures
2. Patient accepts quotation
3. Convert to invoice
4. Patient pays partially
5. Set up payment plan for balance
6. Track installments
7. Complete payment

Validation Rules

Invoice number: Auto-generated, unique
Due date >= Invoice date
Line items: Minimum 1 required
Total: Subtotal + Tax - Discount
Payment amount: > 0, <= outstanding balance
Partial payment: Must update remaining balance
Procedure cost: Must be > 0
Multiple payments: Each reduces outstanding balance
Overpayment: Auto-create credit note

Invoice Status Workflow
Draft → Issued → Partially Paid → Paid
                 ↓
              Overdue (if due_date passed)

Draft → Cancelled (if patient cancels)
Payment Statuses

pending - Not yet processed
completed - Successfully recorded
failed - Failed transaction
partial - Not covering full balance
overpayment - Amount exceeds balance

Invoice Statuses

draft - Not sent to patient
issued - Sent to patient
partially_paid - Some payments received
paid - Fully paid
overdue - Past due date and not paid
cancelled - Cancelled by admin
